How to Make a Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

  1. Grab 2 slices of bread
  2. Grab a jar of peanut butter
  3. Grab a jar of jelly
  4. Grab a butter knife, but remember safety first!
  5. Open both jars
  6. With the knife in hand, scoop a medium amount of peanut butter from the jar (more if you're a peanut butter fanatic) and spread it onto 1 of the slices of bread, taking care to cover the full side-up slice with the peanut butter
  7. Next, scoop a medium amount of jelly from the jar (more if it suits you) and spread it onto the 2nd slice of bread, taking care to cover the full side-up slice with the jelly
  8. Grab both slices of bread and bring them together so that the content-side of each slice mates to each other while also being perfectly aligned
  9. Finally, enjoy!
